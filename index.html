Our Mission Against Drugs

The Kerala Police Department is committed to combating drug abuse and trafficking throughout the state. Our comprehensive approach includes enforcement, prevention, education, and rehabilitation.

We work closely with schools, communities, and healthcare providers to create awareness about the dangers of drug use and to provide support to those affected by addiction.

Learn More About Our Initiative

About Us

Our Mission

Our mission is to eliminate drug abuse from our society by promoting awareness, enforcing laws strictly, and empowering youth with better choices through education and community support.

Our Vision

We envision a drug-free Kerala where communities thrive in safety, health, and well-being, fostering a future where individuals are empowered to lead fulfilling and substance-free lives.

Campaign Overview

Our campaign employs a multi-faceted approach to tackle drug abuse and trafficking in Kerala, focusing on key areas of intervention.

Enforcement & Surveillance

We implement strict monitoring and enforcement measures to identify and dismantle drug trafficking networks operating within Kerala.

  • Border checkpoints and monitoring
  • Intelligence-led operations
  • Coordination with national agencies

Education & Awareness

We conduct educational programs to raise awareness about the dangers of drug abuse, targeting schools, colleges, and community centers.

  • School and college programs
  • Public awareness campaigns
  • Parent education workshops

Rehabilitation Support

We partner with rehabilitation centers to provide support services for individuals struggling with drug addiction.

  • Referral to treatment centers
  • Follow-up care coordination
  • Family support services

Community Engagement

We involve local communities in our anti-drug efforts, empowering citizens to take ownership of the issue within their neighborhoods.

  • Neighborhood watch programs
  • Community policing initiatives
  • Volunteer engagement

Youth Empowerment

We focus on empowering young people with the knowledge and skills to make healthy choices and resist peer pressure.

  • Student ambassador programs
  • Life skills training
  • Peer counseling initiatives

Research & Monitoring

We conduct ongoing research to understand drug abuse trends in Kerala and continuously monitor the effectiveness of our interventions.

  • Data collection and analysis
  • Program evaluation
  • Strategy refinement
